Key Technology

Meet TurboQuant™
The engine behind the pocket.

Inspired by research in KV-cache compression and efficient quantization — TurboQuant lets NeuroPocket AI fit bigger, smarter models into the limited RAM of a real phone, without quality collapse.

🔬

KV Cache Compression

Reduces the memory overhead of the attention cache during inference — the biggest RAM bottleneck in transformer-based models on mobile hardware.

📱

Device-Adaptive Quantization

Instead of one-size-fits-all compression, TurboQuant maps optimization levels to your device profile — 4GB, 6GB, 8GB+ — so quality scales intelligently.

⚙️

5 Tunable Levels — No ML Jargon

Off → Mild → Balanced → Aggressive → Extreme. Users control their experience. The app handles the complexity.

🚀

More Capability, Less RAM

Models that previously needed flagship hardware now run comfortably on mid-range phones — unlocking real AI utility for more users.

What It Does

One app. Five AI superpowers.

Not a demo. Not a proof of concept. A real, everyday AI workspace built for Android — with every core feature running locally on your device.

💬
Private AI Chat
Talk to a capable AI model directly on your phone for writing, brainstorming, Q&A, and summarisation. No prompts leave your device — ever.
📄
Document Q&A
Upload PDFs, TXTs, and CSVs and ask questions about them on-device. Your sensitive files never get uploaded to a server. Context-aware retrieval, built-in.

Vision AI
Capture an image or pick one from your gallery and get instant on-device analysis. Scene description, visual Q&A, context extraction — all local.

Voice Features
